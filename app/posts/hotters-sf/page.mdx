### Activities
- Walk by the water
- Small hike at Lands End by the water, beautiful views
- Hike Muir Woods: beautiful redwood trees, some of the tallest trees in the world, Daddy-friendly hike
- Visit Palace of Fine Arts
- Visit Golden Gate Park
- Lombard Street: the "crookedest street in the world"
- Mission Dolores park
- Fort Mason park
- Bike ride by the water
- Visit Stanford and Palo Alto
- Take a Waymo
- Visit Twin peaks: beautiful view of the city
- Take a ferry to Alcatraz
- Visit UC Berkeley, go on a hike at the nearby Redwood Regional Park
- Go to the top of Coit Tower (beautiful view of the city)
